Why I chose the MILR

Certificate Programs

Cornell ILR offers the most comprehensive array of certificates available to professionals in the workplace. Choose from a variety of certificate programs of study, each delivering a comprehensive series of workshops carefully selected to provide you with in-depth expertise in your chosen field. Select from one of the Certificate Programs below:

  • Diversity Management Certificate
  • Cornell Certified Diversity Professional /Advanced Practitioner Certification
  • Advanced Employee Relations and Investigations Certificate
  • EEO Professionals Certificate
  • Human Resource Management Certificate
  • Contract Administration Studies Certificate
  • Collective Bargaining Studies Certificate
  • Management Certificate

Workshops and Units Needed

Most certificates require at least 60 units. See each certificate's detail page for specific requirements.

  • one-day workshop = 6 units
  • two-day workshop = 12 units
  • three-day workshop = 18 units

Online Learning

Cornell ILR partners with eCornell - Cornell's global learning solutions company - to offer you convenient online training and professional development. Earn a certificate, take individual courses, become certified as a Cornell Certified HR Director and more. You'll receive the same high-quality learning you have come to expect from Cornell ILR.

Human Resources Management

Building a better business requires finding a better option for your human resources management courses. This is why eCornell has developed programs for human resource management online to their exacting standards. Make it happen on your terms with Ivy League excellence and online convenience. eCornell offers human resource management training for management and professional skills development from Cornell's School of Industrial and Labor Relations.
